For amateur and professional rabbit breeders alike. 4H and other Youth Handlers welcome!

Every year, the hamlet of Booville welcomes hundreds of rabbit breeders and their prized stock to its Town Hall field for an upstate New York celebration of rabbit excellence. Competition classes include: Best Bunny in Show, 10 Nationally certified Breeds Confirmation, Best Handler (youth and adult classes), Open Bunny Talent, Open Bunny Speed, Open Bunny Freestyle. Join us for the fun and witness spectacular competition this August !
